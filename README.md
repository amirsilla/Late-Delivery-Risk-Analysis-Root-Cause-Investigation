Project Title:
Late Delivery Risk Analysis and Root Cause Investigation

Business Problem:
Why is the late delivery risk exceeding 54% and what factors are causing delivery delays?

Tools Used:
- Python
- Pandas
- Jupyter Notebook
- Matplotlib

Key Findings:
- First Class shipping showed 95.32% late delivery risk.
- Central Africa had the highest regional delivery risk.
- Golf Bags & Carts had the highest category-level delivery risk.
- More than 20M revenue was associated with delayed deliveries.

Business Recommendations:
- Review First Class logistics operations.
- Improve delivery performance in high-risk regions.
- Monitor high-risk product categories.
